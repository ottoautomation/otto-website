What You Get With Your Dashboard

The OTTO Dashboard gives you complete visibility into your chatbot's performance:

  • Total Leads: Track every lead captured by your chatbot
  • Bookings: Monitor all appointments and reservations
  • Conversion Rate: See how leads convert to bookings
  • Revenue Tracking: Total revenue from booked services
  • Lead Sources: Visual chart showing where leads come from
  • Top Questions: See what your customers are asking most

Features by Tab

Overview: Quick metrics, lead source chart, and recent leads at a glance.

Leads: Full list of all captured leads with contact info, status, and source.

Bookings: All appointments with customer details, service, date, time, and value.

Questions: Most frequently asked questions ranked by popularity.
