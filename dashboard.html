Everything You Need to Know, At a Glance

The OTTO Dashboard puts all your chatbot data in one easy-to-understand interface. No complex analytics tools needed - just clear insights that help you grow your business.

Key Metrics Tracked

  • Total Conversations: See how many visitors are engaging with your chatbot daily, weekly, and monthly
  • Leads Captured: Track every name, email, and phone number collected by OTTO
  • Appointments Booked: Monitor scheduled meetings and consultations (Pro & Elite)
  • Response Time: Average time OTTO takes to respond to visitor questions
  • Resolution Rate: Percentage of conversations successfully handled without human intervention
  • Peak Hours: Discover when your visitors are most active to optimize staffing
  • Popular Questions: See the most frequently asked questions to improve your FAQs
  • Conversion Rate: Track how many conversations turn into qualified leads

Lead Details

Every lead captured includes:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone)
  • Full conversation transcript
  • Timestamp and duration
  • Traffic source (if available)
  • Custom fields you've configured
  • Lead score and qualification status (Elite)

Export & Integration

Your data works where you work:

  • CSV Export: Download leads and reports for use in any spreadsheet
  • CRM Integration: Automatic sync with HubSpot, Salesforce, and more (Pro & Elite)
  • Zapier Connection: Connect to 5,000+ apps for custom workflows
  • Email Reports: Automated weekly summaries delivered to your inbox
