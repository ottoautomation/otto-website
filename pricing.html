Pricing Questions

Are there any hidden fees?

No. The setup fee and monthly cost are all you pay. No surprise charges.

What if I outgrow my plan?

Upgrade anytime! We prorate the difference. Downgrading works the same way.

What payment methods do you accept?

Stripe handles all payments (credit cards, ACH, Apple Pay, Google Pay).

Can I cancel?

Yes. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled anytime with 30 days notice. Setup fees are non-refundable as they cover the custom build of your bot.

Do you offer annual plans?

Yes! Save 20% with annual billing. Use the toggle at the top of the pricing section to see annual rates.
